What is silicone render?

Silicone render is a thin-coat (3–5mm) finishing system applied over a base coat with embedded fibreglass mesh. Its hydrophobic surface makes rain bead and run off, carrying dirt with it — which is why it stays cleaner and resists the green algae and staining common on older finishes across Surrey.

It's through-coloured, meaning the pigment runs right through the topcoat, so it never needs repainting. Combined with a 25–40 year lifespan and near-zero maintenance, it's the most popular premium choice for homeowners in Guildford and the surrounding area.

Benefits of silicone render

  • Self-cleaning & hydrophobic — rain washes dirt away, facades stay cleaner for longer
  • Breathable — lets trapped moisture escape, helping with damp on solid walls
  • Flexible & crack-resistant — the mesh-reinforced base coat absorbs movement
  • Through-coloured — never needs repainting
  • 25–40 year lifespan with minimal maintenance

Silicone render cost

Installed silicone render typically runs £60–£120/m² in Guildford and Surrey, with through-coloured systems at the upper end. A semi-detached home generally lands around £6,000–£11,000 and a detached £11,000–£16,500 including scaffolding. We confirm a fixed written price after a free survey.

Approved application

We're trained applicators for leading silicone systems including K-Rend and Weber. Using the system correctly — base coat, mesh, beads, primer and topcoat — is what preserves the manufacturer warranty and delivers a flawless, lasting finish.
