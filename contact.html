Specialists in render

Guildford Render is a house rendering company based in Guildford, serving Surrey. We focus on external render and wall insulation — silicone, monocouche, K-Rend, Weber, acrylic and EWI — rather than spreading across general building. That focus is why our finishes are consistent, our prep is thorough, and our facades last.

One team, survey to scaffold-down

From the free survey to the moment the scaffold comes down, your project stays with one team. We measure, specify, prep, render and finish — with one point of contact throughout and a fixed price agreed before we start.

Approved applicators

We're trained, approved applicators for K-Rend and Weber systems. Applying a premium render to specification is what preserves the manufacturer warranty and delivers the flawless, decades-long finish these systems are capable of.

What we stand for

  • Honest advice — the right system for your walls, not the priciest
  • Fixed written quotes — system, colour and finish specified upfront
  • Proper prep — we fix damp and hollow render, never cover it up
  • £5M insured with full scaffold and working-at-height cover
  • Warranty-backed — workmanship guarantee plus manufacturer product warranty

Across Guildford & Surrey

We cover everywhere within about 15 miles of Guildford — Woking, Godalming, Leatherhead, Dorking, Cobham, Epsom, Reigate, Cranleigh and Farnham included.
