K-Rend and Weber systems

K-Rend and Weber (both part of Saint-Gobain) make the UK's most widely-specified through-coloured render systems. They're hybrid silicone-monocouche products — combining the through-colour and durability of monocouche with the hydrophobic, self-cleaning properties of a silicone topcoat. The result is a vibrant, long-lasting facade that needs only an annual wash.

Spray-applied for speed and finish

These systems can be spray-applied, which gives a fast, consistent build and a beautifully even finish across large wall areas. As approved applicators we follow the full specification — base coat, fibreglass mesh, beads, primer and topcoat — which is exactly what keeps the manufacturer warranty valid.

Why it matters who applies it

A premium system only performs if it's applied correctly. Incorrect prep, thickness or curing voids the warranty and shortens the life of the render. Using trained, approved applicators protects both your investment and the manufacturer cover that can run for decades.

Cost

K-Rend systems typically sit at £55–£80/m² installed, Weber slightly lower at £50–£75/m². Fixed written quote after a free survey.
