When render needs repair

Render fails for a few common reasons: cracking from movement or poor detailing, 'hollow' or drummy sections where it's lost its bond, blown patches, and green algae or staining on older non-self-cleaning finishes. Left alone, cracks let water in behind the render and the problem spreads.

What we do

  • Diagnose the cause — we tap-test for hollow areas and check for damp behind the render
  • Hack off failed render — removing drummy, cracked or blown sections back to sound substrate
  • Make good — repair the wall and address any damp source before re-rendering
  • Re-render — in a matching or upgraded through-coloured system
  • Pebbledash removal — strip tired pebbledash and replace with smooth modern render

Why fix it properly

Patching over a failing render without addressing why it failed just hides the problem. We find the cause — often a hollow bond or a damp issue the previous installer plastered over — and put it right, so the new finish lasts.

Cost

Repairs are priced per job after survey, depending on the extent of removal and making-good. Full re-renders follow the per-m² rates for your chosen system. Always a fixed written quote first.
