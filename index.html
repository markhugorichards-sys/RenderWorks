What We Apply

Every render system.
One specialist team.

From premium self-cleaning silicone to full external wall insulation, we supply and apply the right through-coloured system for your home — finished to a standard that lasts decades.

Silicone Render

Premium thin-coat, through-coloured silicone render. Self-cleaning, hydrophobic and crack-resistant — rain beads off and washes dirt away. 25–40 year lifespan, never needs repainting.

Monocouche Render

Single-coat, through-coloured render scratched to a clean, uniform finish. Durable and low-maintenance with the colour running right through — no repainting, ever.

K-Rend & Weber Systems

External Wall Insulation

Acrylic & Coloured Render

How It Works

Three steps. No surprises.

A clear process from first contact to completed installation. No obligation to proceed after your free survey.

1

Free Survey & Quote

We visit, measure the wall area, assess the substrate and check for damp or hollow sections. You receive a fixed written quotation with the system, colour and finish specified — no hidden extras.

2

Supply & Installation

Scaffold up, old render hacked off where needed, beads and fibreglass mesh set, base coat applied, then the through-coloured topcoat sprayed or hand-floated to your chosen finish and colour.

3

Aftercare & Maintenance

Scaffold struck, site left clean, and your new facade handed over with a written workmanship guarantee backed by the manufacturer's product warranty on approved systems.

It depends on your walls and goals. Silicone is the premium all-rounder — self-cleaning, breathable and crack-resistant. Monocouche and K-Rend give a durable through-coloured finish. EWI adds insulation. Acrylic suits bold colours and movement-prone walls. We advise honestly at the free survey.
